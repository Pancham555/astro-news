## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, tablet, or mobile phone when you visit a website or use an application. They are widely used to make websites and apps work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" cookies.
* **Session cookies** are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser or application.
* **Persistent cookies** remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them manually.
